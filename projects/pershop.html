Pershop

An iPhone app to manage your personal shopper client list

Pershop (short for "Personal Shopper")

Are you a personal shopper? Would you like a better way to manage your client list? Then give Pershop a try!

  • Link clients to entries in your address book
  • Keep track of dress, shoe, pant, and shirt sizes
  • Take photos of receipts to track previous purchases

Whether you work at a high end clothing store, or do contract work, Pershop will help you manage your clients. Have outfits picked out before the client arrives! Know what they purchased in the past to help find complementary pieces! The happier your clients are the happier you will be!
